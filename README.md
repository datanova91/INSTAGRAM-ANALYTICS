# INSTAGRAM-ANALYTICS
An overview of influencer follower distribution across categories and countries for audience insights.

CASE STUDY

Objective

To understand audience distribution, influencer categories, and country-level engagement patterns.

Key Insights

1. Top Categories by Followers

-Music is the highest-followed category.

-Cinema & Actors, Lifestyle, Sports also attract large follower bases.

-Lower-following categories include Fitness, Modeling, and Art.

2. Country-wise Followers

-United States has the highest influencer follower count.

-India and Indonesia rank second and third.

-Brazil, Spain, Argentina, and Italy follow with smaller shares.

3. Category Distribution

-66.7% influencers belong to a small number of top categories.

-India-specific influencers show strong performance in Music, Cinema, and Sports.

Business Recommendations

-Brands should focus campaigns in the US, India, and Indonesia for maximum reach.

Music and Entertainment categories provide the highest ROI for influencer marketing.

Underperforming categories can target niche audiences via micro-influencers.
